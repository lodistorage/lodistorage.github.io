About Us

Locally Owned

Lodi Storage has proudly served Medina County with reliable storage units since 1998. Our locally-owned facility offers secure, affordable storage solutions to meet both residential and commercial needs. We take pride in maintaining clean, well-maintained storage units in various sizes to accommodate everything from household items to business inventory.

Secure, Accessible Storage Solutions

Whether you need a small storage unit for seasonal items or a larger storage space for furniture during a move, Lodi Storage offers flexible options at competitive rates. Our convenient 24/7 access ensures you can retrieve your belongings whenever needed. From short-term rentals during transitions to long-term storage solutions, we're committed to meeting your storage needs with professional service and attention to detail.
